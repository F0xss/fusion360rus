Specifies how to find the stock that is remaining:

From previous operations takes into account the stock removed by previous operations and machines what is remaining. Recommended to use when previous operations have used a variety of tools. Any edits to previous operations after this toolpath is generated would invalidate this toolpath.

From previous tool finds all material that was unreachable by a previous tool. Recommended to use when previous operations have used similar tools.

Tip: On the Manufacture workspace toolbar, click Inspect > Minimum Radius Analysis to see what areas are accessible by different-sized tools.
