Specifies the thickness of the unmachined material to be machined or ignored, depending on the Adjustment option selected.

With Machine Cusps selected, the Adjustment Offset is the minimum thickness of unmachined material that gets included in the toolpath.

With Ignore Cusps selected, the Adjustment Offset is the maximum thickness of unmachined material that gets ignored by the toolpath.

With Use as Computed selected, the Adjustment Offset works the same way as Machine Cusps when a positive value is entered, or the same way as Ignore Cusps when a negative value is entered.
