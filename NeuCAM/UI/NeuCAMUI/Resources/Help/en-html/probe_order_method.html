Lets you choose the order that the selected holes or bosses are probed.

You can have the holes or bosses probed in the order that you selected them or choose an optimized order that minimizes the distance that the probe travels. You can also choose to probe holes or bosses from the center and outwards or probe them in the X or Y direction. If you select or deselect a hole or boss, the order is automatically updated.
