Pecking forces the tool to cut into the material by the specified Pecking depth distance, gradually progressing down to the finished depth required. The tool then retracts along its path by the specified Pecking retract distance.

Use Pecking disabled

3mm Pecking Depth

Tip: You can easily see the points where the tool is pecking and retracting, by enabling Show points in the Toolpath group while in Simulation.
