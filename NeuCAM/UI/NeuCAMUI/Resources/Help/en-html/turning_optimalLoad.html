The maximum amount of engagement the Adaptive toolpath should maintain.

This can be considered the stepover amount, but Adaptive High Speed Machining will vary the stepover to reduce overloading the tool.

Traditional toolpaths can overload the tool in corners. Adaptive Clearing results in faster material removal and eliminates spikes in tool engagement that could break cutters.

Optimal Load 1.5mm

Optimal Load 3.0mm

Note: This value must be less than the tool radius.
