In conjunction with the Turning Mode, this setting determines the tool cutting direction.

Horizontal Passes

Vertical Passes

Back Cutting

Horizontal - Cuts from the front side of the stock toward the back side. Toward the main spindle.

Vertical - Cuts from the outside, toward the centerline for OD machining. Cuts from centerline, toward the outside for ID machining.

Back Cuttings - For tools with special geometry where chip thinning control is important. Cuts from the back side, toward the front side of the stock.
