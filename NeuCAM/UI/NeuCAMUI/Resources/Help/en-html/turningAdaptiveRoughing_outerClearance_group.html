Shown in Orange, this controls the radius where the tool moves to at the start and end of the toolpath. The tool approaches and retracts from outside the stock along the Z axis to this radial clearance offset. Tip: Click the colored bar and drag.

Outer = Stock OD
Clearance = Outer + 5 mm Offset.

Note: The Clearance radius must be larger than, or equal to, the Outer radius to generate a valid toolpath.
