Maintains the tool-axis tilt at a preferred angle wherever possible.

When machining a steep wall, if a tool is constantly changing its tilting angle unnecessarily, it can leave behind undesired marks on the part. Enable Override Tilt Angle and specify a Preferred Angle to help maintain a constant tilt angle to obtain a better surface finish and reduce the risks of collisions.

No Override
Tool tilts freely

Override Tilt Angle used
Tool stays at a Preferred Angle

Note: The tool stays at the Preferred Angle and may only change its tilt angle when it is necessary to avoid a collision (if Collision Avoidance is enabled).
