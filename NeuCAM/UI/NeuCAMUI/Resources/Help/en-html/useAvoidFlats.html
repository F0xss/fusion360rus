Automatically detects and avoids generating a toolpath over flat areas of the model. Flat areas are detected when they are perpendicular to the Z axis of the tool orientation.

You may want to avoid machining flat areas if you are using a tool that is not suited for flat areas, such as a ball end mill. The flat areas can be machined by a future operation with a more suitable tool, such a flat end mill.
