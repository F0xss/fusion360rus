Specifies the amount of material each tooth removes per spindle revolution.

Cutting with a feed per tooth based on the tool manufacturer's recommendations helps remove material at an efficient rate. A feed per tooth that is too high can lead to tool breakage. A feed per tooth that is too low can cause excess vibrations and dull the sharp edges of the tool.
