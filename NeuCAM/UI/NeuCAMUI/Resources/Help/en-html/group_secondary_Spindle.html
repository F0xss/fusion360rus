These parameters position the subspindle over the workpiece in the main spindle.

The subspindle chuck command can be used to support a workpiece during a cutoff, support a part to reduce vibration, perform a bar-pull operation, or to transfer the part between spindles.

Subspindle positioned over the part.

The subspindle rapids from its home position to the Feed Plane reference position, moves over the part at the specified feedrate to the Chuck plane and clamps the part.
