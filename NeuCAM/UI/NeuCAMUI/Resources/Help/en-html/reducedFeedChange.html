Specifies the maximum angular change allowed in the corner before the feedrate is reduced. The value should be between 0 and 180 degrees.

In the example below, when 90 degrees is specified, no change in the feedrate occurs as all corners are 90 degrees, or smaller than 90 degrees. However, when 89 degrees is specified, corners greater than 89 degrees have their feedrate reduced.
