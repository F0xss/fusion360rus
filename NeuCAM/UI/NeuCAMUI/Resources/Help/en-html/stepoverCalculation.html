Determines how to calculate the stepover of the toolpath.

On surface - Uses the surface of the model to calculate the stepover. This option can detect and machine undercuts.

From tip of tool - Considers the tool's cutting geomtry when calculating the stepover. This option is useful for machining areas where the tool cannot contact all the points on a surface because of the shape of its cutting geometry. For example, an area where the internal corner radius is smaller than the tool's radius.

Using this option lets you use the Spiral option for continuous machining.

Note: From tip of tool is not suitable for machining undercuts.
