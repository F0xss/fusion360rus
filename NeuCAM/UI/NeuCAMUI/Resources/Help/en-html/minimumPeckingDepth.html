As the the Pecking Depth is reduced by the reduction amount, This value specifies a minimum amount to drill per peck.

Shown with a 4mm Minimum Depth

Example: Peck Depth of 8mm, a Pecking Depth Reduction of 2mm and a Minimum Pecking Depth of 4mm. The first peck will be 8mm. The second peck will be 6mm. The third peck will be 4mm and all subsequent pecks will be 4mm.
