Specifies the minimum diameter that holes and circular pockets need to have for them to be included in the selection.

The Minimum corner radius excludes holes and circular pockets that are not reachable by the tool, after which the Minimum hole diameter limit can be used to determine which holes or circular pockets you want to include in the selection.

In the example below, the hole in the top-left corner of the part is not included because its radius is smaller than the minimum corner radius limit.
