Generates a toolpath that resembles either the Pocket or Parallel strategy.

Pocket - Useful for machining complex geometry, such as multiple circular regions. Passes are offset from one another. Options are available to optimize the toolpath over open pockets and generate a spiral-style toolpath for continuous machining.

Parallel - Useful for machining simpler geometry, such as long rectangular regions. Passes are arranged in straight lines and do not have corners. Corners in the toolpath often slow down the machine and may cause marks on the surface.
