Specifies the style of the machining passes for better control.

Spiral - Machines the part in a continuous cut along the length of the part. To ensure a clean finish, a full circle is cut at the two ends.
Line - Moves the tool along the rotary axis in straight lines, with the rotary axis used only at the end of each pass to reposition the part.
Circular - Rotates the part with the tool at a fixed position, effectively machining a circle. The tool then steps over the required amount and machines the next circle.
