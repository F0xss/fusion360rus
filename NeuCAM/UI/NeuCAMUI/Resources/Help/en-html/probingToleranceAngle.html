Specifies the acceptable value of deviation in angle of the geometric feature. This bilateral tolerance is the amount that the angle of the geometric feature can vary and still be considered in tolerance.

A geometric feature whose deviation is beyond this value is considered out of tolerance. For example, when probing a face using Angle Along Y-Axis, the angle is measured from the face to the plane that is normal to the Y axis.

1 - Plane normal to Y axis.
