Allows the cutting moves to extend beyond the Front Limit by the tool radius.

Image shown with Beyond Front Limit selected, from the Lead and Link Moves group, in the Linking tab.

Deselected

Selected

An example of when you may want to extend the cutting moves slightly beyond the front limit is when you have a large tool with a small stepover. This ensures the first cut of the tool has the right amount of material engagement. If the cutting moves do not go beyond the front limit, the first cut of the tool engages with a large amount of material, which can cause tool damage.
