Defines the radius of the toolpath in inner corners for the final stepdown.

This final value is typically smaller than the minimum cutting radius, which means the tool is forced deeper into sharp corners to remove more material than previous passes. This reduces the amount of material that future operations need to remove.

Set to Zero
The toolpath is forced into
all inside sharp corners

Set to 0.07 in
The toolpath will have a blend
of .070 radius in all sharp corners.
