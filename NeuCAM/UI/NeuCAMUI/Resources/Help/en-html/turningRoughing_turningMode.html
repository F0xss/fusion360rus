This setting determines whether the tool machines axially, radially or from the face, as well as the approach/retract direction.

Outside

Face

Inside

Outside - The tool approaches from/retracts to the outside of the stock and machines axially depending on the Direction setting (below).

Face - The tool approaches from the front and machines radially depending on the Direction setting (below).
Note: This option can be used for ID and OD machining.

Inside - The tool approaches from/retracts to the centerline and machines axially depending on the Direction setting (below).
