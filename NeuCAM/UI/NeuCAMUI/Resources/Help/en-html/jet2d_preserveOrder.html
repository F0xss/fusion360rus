When Selected, the chains/features are machined in the order they were selected.
When unselected, the program optimizes the cut order.

As a default Waterjet toolpaths will always be optimized to machine inside bountaries first (slugs), then machine outside boundaries. This keeps the parts from breaking free and moving during a second or third cut on the same part.

Note: See the Linking - Keep Nozzle Down parameters for more control over the movement between inside cut slugs and outside cut parts.
