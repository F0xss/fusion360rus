Controls the feedrate of the tool when performing a plunge move, which is when the tool moves along its tool axis to move into, or outside, the stock.

Vertical plunge shown in red

Generally, when machining soft materials like plastics and wood and plunging into the stock, you require a slower feedrate than the cutting feedrate. If you are plunging outside of the stock, a faster feedrate than the cutting feedrate can be used.
