In addition to any automatically generated tabs, you can add manual tabs to help secure the part in its position during the machining process.

Click anywhere along the contour to add tabs.

After adding a tab, you can adjust its position by clicking and dragging the tab:

To delete a tab that is manually placed, from the canvas, Ctrl + Left click (windows) or Cmd + Left click (mac) on the tab.

Tip: If you want tabs that are only manually specified, from the Tab Positioning drop-down menu, select Number of tabs and set Tabs per Contour to 0.
