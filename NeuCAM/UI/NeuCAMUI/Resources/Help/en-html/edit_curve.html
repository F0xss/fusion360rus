Click positions over an area of a toolpath to place points that create a polygon.

Once you create a polygon, a preview extrusion appears along the direction of the initial view to help visualize the area of the toolpath it is covering.

Points placed over a toolpath

Preview extrusion of the polygon

Use the Points Editor to help you place your points.

Points Editor

1 - Click Accept to complete creating the polygon.
2 - Click Cancel to undo any changes since the last Accept.
3 - Click Delete to remove the active polygon.
4 - Use Insert Point Mode to add more points by clicking near or on an existing line. You can click and hold to drag the additional points to change the shape of the polygon.
5 - Use Delete Point Mode to click on points to delete them.
6 - Click Restore View to return to the initial view you were on when you first started to place points.
