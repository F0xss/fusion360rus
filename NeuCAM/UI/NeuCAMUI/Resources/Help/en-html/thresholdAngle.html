The angle measured from the horizontal plane that sets the division between the Shallow areas and the Steep areas of the model.

Steep areas with slopes larger than the angle use a Contour style toolpath. Shallow areas with slopes smaller than the angle use Parallel or Scallop style toolpaths. This is the divider between near flat machining surfaces and side machining surfaces.

30 Deg. Threshold

60 Deg. Threshold

Note: This is not a containment boundary, since the tool will approach from outside the selected area.
