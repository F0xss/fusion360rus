Reduces the amount of air cutting in the toolpath, which can often reduce machining time significantly. This applies only to scenarios where there is air underneath the stock to be machined, as shown by the illustration below:

When deselected, the edge of the stock is not detected, and excess air-cutting moves are generated.

When selected, the edge of the stock is detected, and fewer cutting moves are generated.

Deselected
Excess cutting moves

Selected
Reduced air cutting

Important: Enabling this feature can increase the calculation time considerably.
