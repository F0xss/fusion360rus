Pecking creates multiple steps across the length of the cutting direction. Between Pecking Depths the tool retracts along its path by the specified Pecking retract distance. Use this if your material creates long strings of chips or to improve coolant flow to the tool tip.

Use Pecking disabled.
Single cut along the path.

8 mm Pecking Depth.
3 mm Retract between Pecks.

Tip: To see the points where the tool is pecking and retracting. Go to Simulation and under the Show Toolpath group, icon.
