Controls the connection moves between cutting moves when:

  • Moving from one pocket to another
  • Moving from one radial cut to another through a stepdown.

Images shown with a Rapid Distance of 20 mm, an Entry Distance and Exit Distance of 10 mm, and a Safe Distance of 9 mm. Generally, if the safe distance is at a greater height than the entry or exit distance, the tool always performs a full retraction move for better safety.

Note: With each stepdown, the retraction move is at a slightly lower height than the previous retraction move. This is because the retraction move is a specific distance instead of a set height to always retract to.
