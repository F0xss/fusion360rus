Determines, depending on the turning strategy, whether a tool is cutting on the Outside Diameter, Inside Diameter, or Face and the approach and retract direction.

Outside grooving

Inside grooving

Face grooving

Outside grooving - The tool approaches from and retracts toward the outside of the stock and machines the model depending on the Direction setting.

Inside grooving - The tool approaches from and retracts toward the centerline and machines the model depending on the Direction setting.

Face grooving - The tool approaches from and retracts toward the front of the stock and machines the model depending on the Direction setting.
