Specifies the counter bore distance as measured from the Bottom Height position, defined in the Heights tab. Since the tool will feed in a positive Z motion this is a positive value.

A centrifugaly advance insert tool might be used, or a reverse counter bore tool with a quick disconnect can be mounted, after the bore shaft enters the hole.
