Tilts the tool sideways with reference to the direction of travel.

1 - Direction of travel
2 - Sideways tilt direction

You can use a lean angle to tilt the tool axis slightly towards the part to cut with a larger area of the tool. In the example below, cutting with a larger area of the tool lets you use larger stepdown values, resulting in a reduction in machining time.

Lead / Lag Angle = 0
Lean Angle = 0
Stepdown = 0.5 mm

Lead / Lag Angle = 0
Lean Angle = -88
Stepdown = 1 mm
