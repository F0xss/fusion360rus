Sets the maximum angle that the tool axis can deviate from its Primary Mode Tool Axis to provide smooth transitions while moving along the Smoothing Distance. The Smoothing Angle takes effect when there are changes in the machine's A and C, or B and C axes.

This example shows the effect of Smoothing Angle when using a Primary Mode of lead/lag 5 deg, lean 0 deg:

Smoothing Distance = 60 mm
Smoothing Angle = 2 deg
Lead/lag and lean angles can deviate by 2 deg
Sudden change of direction at the corner

Smoothing Distance = 60 mm
Smoothing Angle = 30 deg
Lead/lag and lean angles can deviate by 30 deg
Smoother transition at the corner
