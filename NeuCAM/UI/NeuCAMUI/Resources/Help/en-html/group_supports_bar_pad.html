Adds a wider section of bar where the bar meets the build platform.

The wider section improves bar connection to the build platform as well as bar sturdiness. For a pad to be generated, the Pad Height must be less than 40% of the total height of the supports. For example, if the total height of the supports is 50 mm, the height of the pads must be less than 20 mm.
