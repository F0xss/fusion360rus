Defines the size and shape of the raw stock material, from which a part is created.

In milling, turning or mill/turn, and cutting setups, you generate toolpaths to remove stock. In additive setups, you generate toolpaths to add material to stock. Having an accurate representation of the stock helps to create better toolpaths.

Cylindrical stock

irregular-shaped stock like castings

From preceding setup
Creates a stock that matches the stock defined in the previous setup.
