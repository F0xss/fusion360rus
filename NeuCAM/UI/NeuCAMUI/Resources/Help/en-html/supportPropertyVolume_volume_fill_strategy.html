Defines in which directions the raster is generated. Hatches in X and Y creates a grid, while Hatches Just in Y creates lines in Y.

Creating lines in X can be created by using Hatches Just in Y and setting Rotate by Z to 90 degrees. Filling Strategy is not available when the Filling Type is Hollow.
