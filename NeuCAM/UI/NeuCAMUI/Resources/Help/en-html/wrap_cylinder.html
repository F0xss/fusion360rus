Select a cylindrical surface that matches the geometry you want to generate the toolpath on.

Generally, this provides an accurate toolpath if the walls of the cylindrical pocket align with the edge of the tool.

If the walls of the pocket do not align with the tool, you can choose a different wrap cylinder, or adjust the Offset value to get the desired cut.

If you are using axis substitution, the surface selection needs to be along the rotary axis of your machine. For multi-axis machining, the surface can be along any axis as long as the resulting toolpath positions are reachable by the machine.

Note: The surface selection cannot be a cone or an irregular surface.
