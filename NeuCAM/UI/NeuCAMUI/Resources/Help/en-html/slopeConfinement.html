Contains toolpaths based on a range of specified angles.

0 deg - 45 deg

45 deg - 90 deg

Slope angle confinement is specified by the From Slope and To Slope Angle parameters on the Geometry tab. Angles are defined from 0 deg (horizontal) to 90 deg (vertical).

Only areas equal to or greater than the values in the From Slope and To Slope areas are machined.

Most 3D finishing strategies supports slope angle confinement. One use of slope confinement is to confine a selected toolpath strategy to angles where it works best. For example, Parallel Finish is better suited to shallow areas while Contour Finish is better suited to steep areas.
