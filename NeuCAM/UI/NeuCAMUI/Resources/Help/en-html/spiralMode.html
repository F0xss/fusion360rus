Spiral

Spiral with circles

Concentric circles

Spiral creates a path that spirals out from the center point until it reaches the containment boundary or max radius.

Spiral with circles adds a circular toolpath at the minimum radius and at the maximum radius. The circular toolpath is only added if the minimum radius is larger than 0 and the maximum radius is smaller than the radius of the containment boundary.

Concentric circles produces concentric circle paths that are linked. The resulting paths form a circular pattern rather than a spiral pattern.

Morphed spiral spirals from a shape that is not round.
