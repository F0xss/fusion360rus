Smooths the toolpath to reduce the machining time and improves the surface finish of a part.

Depending on your machine tool, to achieve smoother motion, you can choose to:

  • Reduce the number of points on a toolpath by replacing multiple lines in curved areas with arcs; or
  • Replace old points with new points, which are evenly spaced along the toolpath wherever possible.
