Specifies how the toolpath should handle a collision between the shaft and/or holder and the workpiece.

Disabled

Trimmed

Pull away

Detect tool length

Trimmed - Sections of the toolpath that result in safe distances between the shaft and/or holder being violated are trimmed away.

Pull away - The toolpath pulls away from the workpiece to maintain a safe distance between the shaft and/or holder.

Detect tool length - The tool is automatically extended further out of the holder to maintain the specified safe distance between the shaft and/or holder and the workpiece. A message indicating how far the tool is extended out of the holder is logged.

Fail on collision - The toolpath calculation is aborted and an error message logged when the safe distance is violated.
