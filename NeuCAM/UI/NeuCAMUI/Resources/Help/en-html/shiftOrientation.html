Specifies the orientation angle of the boring bar insert tip.

Ideally, the spindle orientation at 0.0 degrees, will position the insert tip to 3 o'clock. Depending on your tool holder and boring bar, 0.0 degrees may not put the insert in the correct location for an X axis shift. Use this parameter to shift the angle of the insert to the 3 o'clock position.

Shift Orientation 0.0 degrees.
Insert Position is off.

Shift Orientation 180.
Insert is at 3 o'clock.

Note: Not all CNC controls have full support for controlling the spindle orientation. It is important to know how your specific machine cycle works and the stopping position of your tools spindle. This may also require modifications to your postprocessor for proper, safe operation.
