Closed or open boundaries can be machined by selecting Faces, Edges or Sketches. You can remove stock from the inside of a pocket or the outside of a standing boss.

Standing Boss

If you select Faces that have holes or pockets, Adaptive will assume those openings are already cleared and avoid machining them. Use Edge selection for pockets with holes or pockets inside of the pocket. For standing bosses select the outer boundary of the boss and check the Stock Contours option shown below. The toolpath will be calculated between the selected boundary and the outer stock area.
