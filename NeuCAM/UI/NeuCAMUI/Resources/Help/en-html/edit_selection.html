Click a pass or segment in the canvas to select it. Use window or crossing selection to select multiple passes or segments.

Tip: Click and drag from left to right to select all passes or segments that are entirely enclosed by the window (window selection). Click and drag from right to left to select all passes or segments that are crossed by the window (crossing selection).
