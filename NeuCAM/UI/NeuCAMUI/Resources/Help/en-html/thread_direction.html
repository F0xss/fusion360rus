Other Considerations. The cut direction, the type of hole (OD or ID) and the threading hand direction (left or right) have an effect on where the thread milling starts. It is possible to cut from top to bottom or bottom to top, depending on these settings.
Examples:
A right-hand thread on an OD (boss), with a climb cut will mill from the top to the bottom.
A right-hand thread on an ID (hole), with a climb cut will mill from the bottom to the top.

Use the Climb or Conventional Direction parameter to control Top to Bottom or Bottom to Top cut direction.
