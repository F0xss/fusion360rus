Controls the mesh resolution by specifying the number of powder layers grouped together to form the smallest element.

Enter a value between 5 and 25. The higher the value, the larger the voxel (volume element) size and the coarser the mesh.

To ensure mesh convergence, specify a value so that there are at least two elements through the thinnest cross-section of the mesh. A three-part mesh study is required to prove mesh convergence.
