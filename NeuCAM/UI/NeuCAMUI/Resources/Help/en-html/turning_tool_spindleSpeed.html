Spindle Speed is rotary motion

Cutting with a spindle speed that is determined by using the tool manufacturer's recommendations helps ensure a good surface finish on parts. You can enter a spindle speed manually, or let Fusion 360 calculate the spindle speed when you enter a recommended surface speed.

Note: You may want to reduce the spindle speed depending on if you have a low powered machine, lack of coolant, or clamping fixtures that have low clamping pressure.
